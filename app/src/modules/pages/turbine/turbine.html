Automated Inspection Systems Steam Turbine Rotorbore Inspection

Founded in 1996, the principals of Automated Inspection Systems began their careers dating back to Westinghouse and WesDyne International. Combined they have over 50 years experience in the design, development, and field implementation of AUT of steam turbine rotorbore shafts.


A primary vendor of AUT data systems, AIS has proven experience and knowledge to provide our customers with the properly configured data system, the engineering and technical support and the training to allow their personnel to perform their own examinations. Customers who normally purchase our Turbine Rotorbore System normally fall into 1 of 4 categories:

  1. Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs)
  2. Turbine Maintenance and Service Shops
  3. Electric and Power Companies
  4. NDT inspection companies











Summary

AIS provides a complete turnkey Turbine Rotorbore System consisting of Data Acquisition Hardware, Scanner, Scan Controller, Advanced Data Analysis Software, Couplant Tank, Transducers and Wedges. Advantages of the AIS Turbine Rotorbore System are many fold:

  1. One company provides the entire system
  2. Consumables (transducers and wedges) are priced moderately and readily available
  3. Bore head adapts for 2 to 10 inch bore diameters, bottle bore compliant, easily scan bores with multiple diameters
  4. Low Noise design incorporating onboard motor controllers, passive filters and isolation (No slip rings)
  5. Analysis tools and software complies with SAFER and WEORLE fracture mechanics format
  6. On-site customer training and support
  7. AIS provides support and / or an upgrade path for the life of its products
