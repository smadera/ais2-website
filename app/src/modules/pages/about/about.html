Company Profile


Founded in 1996 by former employees of Westinghouse and WesDyne International, the principals of AIS combined have over 50 years of professional experience specializing in Automated UT in and amongst a variety of industries and applications.


AIS personnel include Hardware, Software, Mechanical and NDT professionals. We design, manufacture and provide Automated UT solutions. Past projects and experience include AUT inspection systems for nuclear power pressure vessels, nuclear piping, commercial and fighter jet aircraft wings, steam turbine rotorbores, cross country pipe lines, heavy wall subsea piping, wind turbine blades, standard corrosion mapping and weld examination for new and in-service pressure vessels and piping systems.


All AIS products and systems are designed and manufactured in-house by AIS engineers and personnel. Each system sold can be summarized as follows:


 
  • Transducers
  • Scanning Device (Motorized or Manual)
  • Motion Controller
  • Ultrasonic Instrumentation
  • Acquisition and Analysis Software
  • Cables and Umbilicals
  • Manufacturing
  • Training
  • Post Sales Support
